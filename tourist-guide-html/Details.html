Roman Theater
Amman

Brief about Roman Theater

The theater is built into the side of a hill and has a semi-circular shape, with three tiers of seating and a stage at the bottom. The walls of the theater are decorated with carvings and reliefs, and there are several underground passageways and chambers beneath the stage.
