Who are we

The MENA Academy was established in  2022  and it is one of the companies o f the ISO City Group, which was established in  2014  , as  the MENA  is one of the most important leading academies for training, education and q ualification of i ndividuals and employees in the most important administrative, financial, technical and self-development fields. The Academy offers a comprehensive set of training and development solutions for both individuals and professionals. From companies locally and regionally with the latest academic equipment.

Our Message

Providing educational and training services efficiently, effectively and competently to Jordanian individuals and institutions alike  and empowering them to be competitive leaders locally and globally to qualify them for the local, regional and global labor market, through the adoption of professional, technical and academic programs and certificates with a number of local and international bodies.

Our Stragistic Goals

 Journal of research work published in educational fields according to international standards.
Attracting and qualifying students to join higher education institutions inside and outside the country
Participate in the development of the educational and training system to qualify students and workers in the field for the global market
Develop academic, vocational and technical educational programs to be of international quality.

We believe in collaboration and innovation.

These core values define who we are and the work we do.

Vision

To be the pioneers in the world of vocational, technical and academic training and qualification with the highest standards

Commitment

MENA Academy is committed to providing its services to beneficiaries within the highest academic, professional and technical standards

Respect

MENA Academy treats all parties with respect

Good governance
