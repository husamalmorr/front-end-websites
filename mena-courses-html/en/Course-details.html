THE EXPLANATION

This course aims to develop students' skills in writing paragraphs in English, for academic and professional purposes, and to provide students with education and training to solve problems related to the multiple forms of paragraphs, which employ many means and methods; Such as: cause and effect, comparison and contrast, and definition. The course also focuses on the characteristics of a good paragraph; Such as: unity, interdependence and integration. This course also provides a review of the most important foundations of paragraph writing, and provides students with training in writing narrative and descriptive text.

ADVANTAGES

The English course helps you to become a confident and independent global citizen:
You will work on an exciting range of practical tasks and challenges that encourage self-expression and creative thinking
By working with your colleagues on group tasks, you will enhance your collaboration and leadership skills.
Stay Connected and Continue Your Learning Journey Between lessons
To help you get the most out of each lesson, your teacher will prepare suitable materials for interactive and independent study which means you will be able to come to lessons prepared so you can focus on improving your English speaking skills:
• Everyone learns in their own way, so you'll complete independent, interactive study materials at your own pace, helping you build your confidence in speaking English and prepare for your future
Assignments are designed to develop your reading, writing and listening skills
You will use real English-language resources, such as newspaper articles, music recordings or sports videos, to improve your knowledge of grammar and vocabulary
Your own dashboard will help you work on your projects and track your progress, at any time
You can communicate with your teacher and classmates through the discussion forum Chat with colleagues about the project you're working on and upload what you've accomplished to be reviewed by your teacher.
Be yourself and boost your confidence in a safe and inclusive learning environment
Our safe learning environment provides a space to be yourself:
You will share lessons with other students of the same language level and age
Your teacher will give you plenty of opportunities to express your opinions freely, which Helps you gain confidence in speaking English
Your well-being is our priority, and strict procedures have been put in place to ensure your safety
