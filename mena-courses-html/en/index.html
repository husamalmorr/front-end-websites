Who are we

The MENA Academy was established in 2022 and it is one of the companies of the ISO City Group, which was established in 2014 , as the MENA is one of the most important leading academies for training, education and qualification of individuals and employees in the most important administrative, financial, technical and self-development fields. The Academy offers a comprehensive set of training and development solutions for both individuals and professionals. From companies locally and regionally with the latest academic equipment.
